    Certified by the City of Vienna

    IKI is an accredited examination centre for the SD Austrian German Diploma in accordance with the level placement of the Council of Europe as well as for the IF Integration Exam of the Austrian Integration Fund.

    In the Heart of Vienna

    IKI offers German courses to adults and youth from the age of 16 at a city-central location.

    Connected Worldwide

    Every month 450-500 students from all around the world attend our German courses. IKI is a partner institute of the University of Washington, the NC State University and the University of Kansas.

    Vis vis the State Opera

    IKI Language Academy is located directly across from the Vienna State Opera House. From here, you walk just a few minutes to the most important signature points of the city, such as the Vienna Naschmarkt, Krtnerstrasse, the Graben, St. Stephens Cathedral, or to the several famous Vienna coffee houses in the city.

    Join the pack

    A trip to Schoenbrunn with a tour through the park and visit of the palace.

    The best is saved for last. Chilling out with our After-Class Activities
    IKI makes sure that the entertainment is ongoing for the whole duration of the summer campus programme. After the 3-hour lesson, there is typically an exciting programme awaiting. Here is your chance to get to know both the several highlights of the city and also the participants from the other courses. Joint walks, daytrips, cinema and museum visits, along with dances, communal cooking, and of course our IKI parties offer ample opportunity.

    Vienna has a lot to offer in summer. You can take a stroll at Schoenbrunn, go for a swim at the Danube Island, go cycling, jogging, or skating. The several traditional coffee houses are perfect for meeting up with friends, or for simply doing homework or reading the newspaper.
