Welcome in the Boutique Pension Hargita

On one of the most magnificent streets of Vienna, we welcome you with a pleasant and familiar atmosphere. Stay with us and enjoy one of the most breathtaking cities in Europe. With friendly and well-maintained rooms, our house is ideal for your stay in Vienna.

You would like to discover and get to know Vienna, its magnificent architecture and the famous "Viennese charm"? You would like to taste its delicacies and follow in the footsteps of Freud, Sissi and other famous Viennese personalities?

Then you are in the best hands at our hotel. Centrally located and lovingly furnished we offer you the balance between a lot of new things that Vienna has to offer for you and a relaxing retreating place, where you feel comfortable.

We offer:

  • live centrally, directly on the largest shopping street in Vienna

  • fully equipped apartments & rooms

  • bright, friendly rooms where you can enjoy your time

  • Rich breakfast buffet to start the day perfectly

  • free Wifi

  • room Service
