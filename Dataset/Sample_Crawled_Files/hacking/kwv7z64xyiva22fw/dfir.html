About DFIR
English (USA)

This list is intended to provide SANS Alumni with access to a forum to ask questions related to Digital Forensics, Incident Response, and Reverse Engineering Malware. SANS is dedicated to helping build communities. The digital forensics community is a growing field and it is useful to help grow your knowledge that you invested so much of your time into.

The mailing list is also a perfect place to send out messages for job announcements that are DFIR related. We know how hard it is to find decent individuals who can perform forensics or incident response. We fully endorse the list to be used as a way for those looking to hire someone skilled to their team. Our only requirement is that the posting must be done by a DFIR professional who works in the field, not a recruiter. That way the individual can reach out to the job poster asking specific questions about the job.
Another list guideline that we follow. The list is not intended to push commercial products or services. It is ok to link your website to your signature at the bottom of a good question or reply. It is also ok and encouraged to announce free webcasts, tools, utilities, and content from your organizations as well.

To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the DFIR Archives. (The current archive is only available to the list members.)
