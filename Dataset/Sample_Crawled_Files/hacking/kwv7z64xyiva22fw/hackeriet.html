Welcome to Hackeriet!

Hackeriet is a community operated hackerspace in Oslo where people tinker with software, networks, art and hardware, learn from each other.

hacker: n. [originally, someone who makes furniture with an axe]
A person who enjoys exploring the details of programmable systems and how to stretch their capabilities[..]

The Jargon File

We're on the the ground floor of Hausmania Culture House in Oslo, 20 minutes walk from Oslo S.

When is it open?

Hackeriet is open for everyone Tuesdays and Saturdays from early in the evening till late. Members have access around the clock. Right now there's maybe someone there.

Getting to Hackeriet

Our space is on the ground floor of Hausmania Culture House at Hausmannsgate 34, Oslo.

Enter the exterior door, then use the door bell and we'll come get you. Our space is trough the next door ahead, which is usually locked.

How can I become a member?

First, show up at a couple of open events so we can get to know each other. You don't have to be a member to be included, it's important to us that everyone feels welcome.

Bring your laptop, your project, your ideas, your questions on a problem and your will to learn, and maybe help us solve one of our problems or complete some of our tasks.

In addition to paying dues members get to subscribe to the internal e-mail list and 24/7 access to the space.

When you decide Hackeriet is for you, you fill out a short form and we take care of the details.
